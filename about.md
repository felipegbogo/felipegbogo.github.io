### Short bio

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at **Universidade Federal Fluminense (UFF)**, Rio de Janeiro,  
and from 2025–2026 I am a **visiting student researcher** at **Nanjing University**, Jiangsu, China [file:86].

My current PhD project, tentatively titled *"Yamabe–Escobar Singular Problem"*, explores analytical and geometric aspects of conformal geometry on manifolds with singularities.  
Broadly, I am interested in nonlinear elliptic PDEs in geometry, scalar curvature problems, and geometric analysis [file:86].
