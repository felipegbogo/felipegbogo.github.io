I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics at **Universidade Federal Fluminense (UFF)**, Brazil,  
currently a visiting PhD student at **Nanjing University**, China. My research lies in **Differential Geometry**, with a focus on **Riemannian geometry** and **conformal geometry** [file:86].

I am jointly supervised by **Prof. Sérgio de Moura Almaraz** (UFF) and **Prof. Chen XueZhang** (Nanjing University).  
Previously, I obtained both my **M.Sc.** and **B.Sc.** in Mathematics from **Universidade Estadual de Maringá (UEM)**, Brazil [file:86].

On this website I share:

- Research projects and preprints in differential and conformal geometry.
- Lecture notes and short expository texts.
- Mathematical communications and ideas in progress.
